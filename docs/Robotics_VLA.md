# Robotics VLA Research

## Learning & Develop Steps

1. Configure Isaac Sim & Isaac Lab
2. Evaluate the Baseline Methods for **Locomotion and VLA**
3. Redesign the simulation environments
4. Retrain or finetune the trained models to adapt to new environments
5. Deploy models on robotics for validation
